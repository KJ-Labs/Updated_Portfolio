About me

I'm a aspiring Software Engineer. I currently work for Amazon in the Alexa space as a Senior Business Intelligence Engineer III. My day to day workload is automating the once thought impossible, hunting bad actors in the skills space and working to surface complex information in simple to understand tableau dashboards.


I'm an extremely quick learner. I graduated with my bachelors at age 20 with a 3.903 G.P.A and I earned my masters, with a 3.886 GPA, before I could legally drink (Age 21). I began my career at Goldman Sachs, automating myself out of a job every 6 months with just VBA. The techical bug took hold and after 4 years in the Investment Banking world, I officially turned to tech. I started as a lowly L5 Business Analyst and within 3 years I was promoted to a (L6) Senior Business Intelligence Engineer at Amazon.


I just graduated from the University of Washington's Full-Stack Flex bootcamp. We focused on the MERN stack, but I've learned and dabbled in other languages in my free-time, such as python and java, in addition to expanding the javascript and react skills which you'll see below.
