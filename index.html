About me

I'm a aspiring Software Engineer. I completed my entire undergraduate degree in 2 years and earned Summa Cum Laude with a 3.906 GPA. After college, I began working at Goldman Sachs, it was there I realized how powerful automation and technology was, and how critical it would be to learn.

I began expanding my technical abilities and moved to Washington to become a Business Intelligence Engineer for Amazon Alexa. I've worked for Amazon for nearly 3 years and my SQL, Python, and Data Engineering skills have rapidly expanded, but I'm always looking to become more technical.

Currently I'm working to become a fullstack developer through the University of Washington and continue my pursuit of knowledge. I've learned HTML5, Javascript, CSS, Bootstrap, Node JS, Sequelize, React, Handlebars, ORM, MVC and plan to learn C in the future.

In my free time I like to paint miniatures for my friend's D&D games and sell the extras on Etsy. I'm also working to become proficient in Z-Brush in order to sculpt my own miniatures to print.
